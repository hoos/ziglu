A better business

We will always seek to do things the way they should be done. This means putting our customers before our profits, and being open and honest about everything we do even when it hurts, and especially when we make mistakes.

It’s important to us to be proud of what we do, whether it’s our app or our company. We’re trying to build something different (and a bit special), not just replicate what others have done in the market. Something that doesn’t just deliver what customers have long wanted, but also something that truly reflects our values and what we stand for. In being this ambitious, you don’t always get everything 100% right first time, however hard you try. What we will do 100% though is listen. We will be guided by your feedback, and together we’ll build something beyond what we can imagine today.
